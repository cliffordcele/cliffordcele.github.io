Projects

School Survey, Economic Need, & Test Scores

A factor analysis is conducted with the student results from the NYC School Survey to determine what factors underlie student achievement. Then linear regression is used to investigate the association between those factors and the mean English Regents score for each high school while considering its economic need index.

Skills: R (RStudio), Linear Regression, Principal Component Analysis (PCA), Factor Analysis
Publication: Research Article

Predicting the Future Prices of Cocoa Beans

This time series analysis project attempts to predict the future prices of cocoa beans on the ICE Contract Market using ARIMA and GARCH models.

MiBici: Guadalajara's Bike-Sharing System & COVID-19

This exploratory data visualization analysis illustrates how the COVID-19 pandemic changed the way people use the MiBici bike-sharing system in Guadalajara, Mexico.

Heart Disease & the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System

The goal of this project is to use classification techniques to investigate which factors/covariates are important to predict whether a person will get a coronary heart disease (CHD) or myocardial infarction (MI) with data from the US BRFSS telephone survey.

Skills: Python, Scikit-learn, Pipelines, Machine Learning, Supervised Learning, Classification.
Publication: Report & Presentation
